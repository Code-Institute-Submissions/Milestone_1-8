Why Learn Russian?

Boost your mental power

Did you know that learning a second language is beneficial for your mental health? Researchers have found that being able to speak a second language changes the way your brain operates and improves your cognitive skills (e.g., memory and decision making). Learning Russian can definitely boost your brain! I offer a truly personalized learning experience by preparing an individual learning program for you to keep your brain entertained. You can learn Japanese and travel to Japan or master Italian and visit the country. It's not as hard as you think

At first glance Russian can be a scary sight. However, as Russian has a phonetic alphabet, after learning the sounds of 33 letters you can already start reading and writing. Russian grammar and pronunciation pose challenges of a higher rank but with some dedication, I've seen different students reaching fluency. For those who can speak any of the Slavic languages, Russian grammar is quite easy. But don't be afraid my non-Slavic friend, with some dedication you can get there too.
