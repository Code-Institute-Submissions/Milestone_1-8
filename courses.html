Types of lessons

Private Lessons

I develop an individual program for each student. If you choose individual lessons, you can benefit from your development program 100%. We will dedicate some time to set up your goals and milestone so you can see and measure your progress. We can discuss topics of your interest and work on the weak points. Be ready to motivate yourself and do your homework each time we have a lesson.

Price: 30 per 1 hour

Skype Lessons

If you prefer the convenience of Skype lessons, make sure that you have all technology set up for it. Same as for individual lessons, we will create your learning program with goals and milestones. You will be mostly working on Google Classroom where I will be publishing your homework, additional exercises, and tests. You can shape the content and the frequency of these lessons.

Price: 20 per 1 hour

Group Lessons

The group lessons are available for those who are willing to travel to The Hague city center. There are usually not more than 5 people in each group with a similar level of Russian. You will receive all study materials according to your level, so you won't need to worry about buying books. At the beginning of each level, we will set up individual and group goals, which we will be tracking together until the end of the course.

Price: 20 per 1 hour
